Photography

I love photography, and had the privilege of winning an Honorable Mention for the Kennedy Center for International Studies Photo Contest of 2021-2022. While not technically trained in photography, it's a passion of mine that requires precision and attention to detail. Below is the photo I entered.

Discongruity - This photo was taken through the window of a high-rise office space in São Paulo. Each of the buildings visible through the window were constructed in a different era of architecture and are in varying levels of weathering and age. Mid-century-modern, utilitarian, mission revival, and modernist buildings scrape the sky with crumbling concrete, stucco, and tinted glass. Coupled with balconies, windows, and AC units is a glimpse of mundane city-life through the decades. In these contrasts I see the personality, resiliency, and work-ethic of Brasileños. I will always cherish my two short weeks in Brasil and am so grateful for the opportunity to share it!

Thrifting

One other hobby of mine is thrifting. I love being intentional about life, and giving an old thing a new home is so satisfying. The attention to detail and invested time, the moment where you fine a piece with character and personality; there is a reward to it. That is part of why I love coding, design, and marketing. They all provide an opportunity for me to bring life, character, and personality to a brand.
